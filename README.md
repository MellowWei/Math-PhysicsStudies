## Boundary Statement · 边界声明

**Math-Physics Studies ≠ traditional mathematical physics.** This discipline is not the Western academic field of mathematical physics (which uses differential equations, group theory, etc., to formulate physics problems). It is named by the sum of four characters in their **character-root** meanings, not in the compound-word "mathematics + physics" reading. Same name, entirely different domain.

**"物" and "理" do not overlap with Real Physics.** In MP-0, 物 = "concrete material thing" and 理 = "intrinsic pattern." These are *not* the same as the 物理 of Real Physics (which is vibration-as-physical-thing-body per P-2, and necessary product of time and space per P-13). **Same characters, different precise meanings, different disciplines.**

**Not the same as "数学物理学" from earlier conversations.** Productions in other conversation threads using the same name (featuring nine-operator hypothesis, generative chain, EVF-TI framework) are independent works. They do not constitute this discipline's content. **This discipline begins with MP-0 and contains only what MP-0 contains.**

**Compactness ≠ insufficiency.** One axiom, four characters — fully founded. MP-1, MP-2 may emerge, but are not required for the discipline to stand.
